Rasam Ramen: A Fusion Worth Slurping?

Rasam Ramen is a bold fusion of South Indian and Japanese comfort foods. It brings together the tangy, spiced broth of rasam with the umami-packed richness of ramen.

Imagine slurping noodles in a tamarind-tomato broth seasoned with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and a hint of miso. Sounds wild? We want to know what you think.
